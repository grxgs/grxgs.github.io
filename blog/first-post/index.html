First Post!
Jan 14, 2017
2 minutes read

Kudos for stumbling upon this blog and more specifically this post. I’m not one for very long worded and fancy intros. So this will be a relatively short post.

This blog has a few purposes. The main purpose is to have somewhere to record my hopefully mildly entertaining life experiences. You probably just asked yourself what type of life experiences might you expect.

Well, this will focus on a two things really. First is my goal towards becoming employed as a software developer. I’ve been involved with technology in some capacity (more than less) for the past 7 years. After trying out a variety of roles, I figured I give software development an actual shot.

Secondly is my fitness journey. As a desk jockey and former Pepsi enthusiast I am not in the best shape I could be. After realizing that it’s easier to get in shape sooner than later figured I start now. As opposed to waiting until I’m in my 40s.

One of the other purposes of this blog is hopefully serve as some motivation (for myself and others)as I progress. As time goes by, it would be great to look back at my former self and see how I’ve changed and improved. Along with that, it would be great if this blog convinced just one other person to do the same.

Besides that, there isn’t much more for me say. The next few posts you’ll see will talk about what I plan on learning and learning plan. Until next time…
